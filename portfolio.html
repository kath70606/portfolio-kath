About

From circuits to systems of a different kind

I graduated with a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Southern Mindanao, where I learned to think in systems — how components connect, where a signal can fail, and how to design for reliability under real constraints.

That mindset carried over when I started my career in internal audit, testing controls and tracing how processes actually behaved versus how they were documented on paper. Today, as a business analyst, I spend my time doing something similar: mapping workflows, reading the data underneath them, and turning what I find into recommendations people can act on.
